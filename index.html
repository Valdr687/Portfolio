About me :

Why walk when you can climb ? Why run when you can fly ? Life is meant to be tasted in three dimensions.

Around 8 or so years I received my first camera, it was an old-fashioned compact, but I didn't stopped me to point it at everything. Trough the years, I've grown accustomed to always have my camera next to me. I would soon find an interest in relief, in the small details who create big panoramas. That's how I became hooked to digital creation, the only unlimited form of art. The only one who allow one to see his creations turning to life, little pixels turning into something beautiful.

It was only natural that I was attracted to Minecraft, that I start playing and creating in this game, through this game. I started with a friend, it's now something I share with thousand of people. What's make Minecraft incredible is the level of personalization possible, I've taken fully advantage of this to make texture packs. Using my faithful pixels, I've created my own experience of Minecraft, for myself and my friends or so I believed. Now my creations have been downloads
